Quest for the Nuno - PlayStation Game

"Quest for the Nuno" is an upcoming RPG game set to release on PlayStation 5 and PlayStation 4, with a digital release on the PlayStation Store (PSN). The game combines the nostalgic charm of classic RPGs with modern gameplay mechanics, offering a unique experience reminiscent of beloved titles like "Pok√©mon," "The Legend of Zelda," and "Dark Souls." Players embark on a fantastical adventure set in the lush and mysterious Philippine Islands, exploring enchanted forests, ancient ruins, and vibrant villages, all while uncovering the secrets of a mythical creature known as the Nuno.

The game features a mix of strategic turn-based combat, open-world exploration, and rich storytelling, providing players with a deeply immersive and rewarding RPG experience. Key gameplay elements include exploration, combat, quests, resource management, character development, and multiple endings based on player choices, encouraging replayability and deep engagement.

Slated for release in Q4 of 2025, "Quest for the Nuno" aims to captivate players worldwide, with a special focus on North America, Europe, and Asia. The game is designed to appeal to fans of classic RPGs, indie game enthusiasts, and players who appreciate games with cultural depth and unique settings.

Number Base Converter - Android App

Number Base Converter is an innovative Android app I developed, available on the Google Play Store. This app, built using the Python Kivy and KivyMD library, allows users to seamlessly convert binary numbers to decimal numbers and vice versa. It provides an intuitive interface and ensures accurate conversions, making it a valuable tool for students, engineers, and anyone dealing with number systems.
